The batterie will release a lot of energy for the drone when it will be power on. This energy will be complicated to handle for an normal switch that's why we need a MOFSET transistor to handle the power switching. This kind of transistor required only a small amount of power to switch instead of the big amount of power needed by a standard switch.
The first thing to do is to cut an aluminum plate piece since I imagine we will have a lot of heat release. Even a good MOSFET has a tiny bit of resistance when it's "on," and at high current. It still makes some heat, so the transistor bolts to the aluminium, which acts as a heatsink.
